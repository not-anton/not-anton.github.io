Privacy Policy

Point-Less is a playful, comic-inspired agile story-pointing app. We respect your privacy and keep things as simple as possible.

What We Collect

  • No personal accounts. You join with a nickname only.
  • No tracking cookies. We do not use analytics or ad trackers.
  • Room data is temporary. All story and pointing data is stored in memory and deleted when the room is empty.

How We Use Data

  • We use your nickname only to show who is in a room.
  • Room codes and stories are only visible to people in your room.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns, email us at hello@point-less.work.
